About Costa Coffee

We are passionate about coffee and believe that every cup tells a story. Our mission is to bring the finest coffee to our customers, creating moments of connection and comfort.

Our Story

Costa Coffee started with a simple goal: to provide the best coffee in the world. From our humble beginnings in London, we've grown into one of the most loved coffee brands in the world, all thanks to our loyal customers and our dedication to crafting the perfect cup.

We pride ourselves on our expertly sourced coffee beans, our commitment to sustainability, and the strong relationships we build with our customers.

Our Values

  • Quality: We always strive for the best in everything we do, from the beans we choose to the drinks we serve.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to protecting the environment by sourcing responsibly and reducing waste.
  • Community: We believe in the power of connection and create welcoming spaces for people to come together.
  • Innovation: We continuously evolve to meet our customers’ needs, offering new and exciting experiences.
